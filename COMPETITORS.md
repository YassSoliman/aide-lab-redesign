## Key Design & UX Trends Identified

### Modern Dental Lab Website Must-Haves

1. **Case Submission Portal**
   - Real-time order tracking
   - Digital scan uploads
   - Direct communication with technicians
   - Notification management
   - Online billing/payments

2. **3D Viewing Technology**
   - HD 3D model viewing
   - Full rotation, zoom, pan
   - Mobile-friendly viewers (iOS/Android)
   - Jawbone visualization (comprehensive view)

3. **Digital File Support**
   - Common 3D file format acceptance
   - Direct scan submissions
   - CT/cephalometric image uploads

4. **Visual Design Trends**
   - Dark mode options
   - Clean, minimalist layouts
   - High-quality dental photography
   - Before/after galleries
   - Video content/virtual tours

5. **Trust Builders**
   - Certifications/partnerships displayed
   - Testimonials with photos
   - Years in business highlighted
   - Team/facility photos
